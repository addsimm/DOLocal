Mission:
We enrich lives - especially older adults'- by delivering a fun, supportive space for meaningful expression, enjoyment and friendship in order to promote healthier minds and happier souls.

Join Our Story is a California benefit organization that helps older adults fill their lives with creative expression, supportive social connections and positive recognition. To reach this goal we deliver an accessible, compelling online platform where people can enjoy, create, and share. The vision includes a slate of dramatic and instructional programming, produced by professionals and supplemented by users

Our Better Storytelling course, which evolved from tests conducted at a range of senior communities, provides one cornerstone. Better Storytelling adapts to welcome every ability level through easy-to-use tools and intriguing assignments that also promote community and friendship. Further support is readily available in order to overcome any initial hesitation and provide boosts as needed. This course has received overwhelmingly positive feedback. In line with Join Our Stories mission, Better Storytelling increases social engagement, heightens mental acuity and, most importantly, is incredibly fun.

Though many writing programs exist, this Join Our Story course is the first specifically devoted to improving older adults' quality of life and to fostering an active, ongoing community. Storytelling always centers on social interaction, to which Join Our Story adds motivational and intellectual support. The Join Our Story team is lead by Celeste Davidson, a multiple award winning author, certified educator who is doing doctoral research in educational technology; it also includes professionals from academia, social services, health and entertainment. Together, we have worked as teachers, scientific researchers, professional writers, and executives in startups and large corporations - some nonprofit. Most of all, we are compassionate individuals dedicated to helping older adults lead creative, connected, and healthy lives.
