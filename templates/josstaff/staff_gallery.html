Our mission:
Join Our Story helps older adults enjoy safe and supportive social connections, create and share meaningful stories, and receive due recognition for their efforts. The goal of our collaborative online storytelling platform is to fight social isolation and maintain cognitive function, bringing better health and happiness to an under-served community.
