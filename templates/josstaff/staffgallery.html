Mission:
We enrich lives - especially older adults'- by delivering a fun, supportive space for meaningful expression, enjoyment and friendship in order to promote healthier minds and happier souls.

In short, to make the world a happier place!
Join Our Story is a new California benefit organization whose mission is to help older adults fill their lives with creative expression, supportive social connections and positive recognition in American culture and society. JOS is pursuing this mission by developing an accessible, compelling online platform where people can enjoy, create, and share content. The vision is to develop a slate of dramatic and instructional programming, produced by professionals and supplemented by users, that fosters engagement.

Our creative storytelling course delivers a cornerstone, which has evolved from tests conducted at diverse senior communities in California (in Burbank and Compton). This course has received overwhelmingly positive feedback and is ready for widespread use in other communities.

The Join Our Story course, in line with its overall mission, helps heighten individuals' social engagement, improve their mental acuity, and increase their enjoyment of daily life. The workshops, exercises and activities on storytelling promote creativity and prosocial behavior. Course pedagogy facilitates a broad range of ability levels through easy-to-use tools and fun assignments that encourage the participants and prompt interaction with others. Further, templates and examples are available to overcome initial hesitation and provide boosts as needed.

Though many writing programs exist, the Join Our Story course is the first developed specifically to enhance the quality of life for older adults and is designed to foster a large and ongoing community. In particular, the storytelling aspect centers on social interaction, which includes motivational and cognitive support. Our curriculum developer is an author and educator with expertise in educational technology; the team also includes professionals from academia, social services, fitness, and entertainment. Our staff has worked as teachers, scientific researchers, professional writers, and leaders of both nonprofit and international organizations. They are compassionate individuals dedicated to the mission of helping older adults and their friends and families lead creative, connected, and healthy lives.
