Our Story

Become part of a vibrant online community for people, especially older adults, who love good stories. At Join Our Story, we guarantee you'll have fun, make friends, and become a confident storyteller, as you take advantage of our many offerings.

  • Enjoy Hollywood Heights
    Our exclusive dramatic series portrays the challanges we face with sensitivity and humor. It also provides a great springboard for discussion and creative inspiration.

  • Create your own stories
    If you like, build confidence and skills in our fun workshops; they're loaded with simple tips and tricks, helpful templates and entertaining prompts. Or, cut to the chase, and create your own characters and plots within the world of Hollywood Heights. Who knows? Your work might come to life in future episodes!

  • Share stories – yours and ours
    Meet interesting people and make new friends as you gossip about Hollywood Heights; drop into writer’s rooms to pitch ideas and work with others; then, publish your stories in our galleries to receive the rave reviews you deserve.
